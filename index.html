ABOUT

A little bit about myself.

My name is Alex. I was born on August 22, 2004. Music has been around me since childhood — from Eminem to Nirvana. Later, I started exploring music on my own and discovered artists like Skrillex, Linkin Park and many others.

At the age of 12, I downloaded FL Studio for the first time — that’s where my journey began.

At first, I tried making electronic music inspired by Skrillex, but quickly realized it wasn’t as easy as it seemed. I then shifted into rap, started making beats, creating type beats, and searching for my own sound. From 12 to 18, I went through many different aliases — each one representing a different stage of my growth as an artist.

At the same time, I was learning to play electric guitar and MIDI keyboard, exploring new plugins, sound design techniques, and gradually diving deeper into the music creation process.

At 18, I earned money from my music for the first time. During that period, I focused heavily on mixing and mastering, took on client work, and improved technically. But something was missing — I wasn’t enjoying it. While others were making dozens of type beats every day and were satisfied with it, I felt like I was losing myself.

I quit music many times and kept coming back to it. Later, inspired by BNYX, I tried switching to Ableton Live — and I’ve been using it ever since.

At the same time, I explored other creative directions: I tried making games, learned video editing, studied programming and web development, customized and even sewed some of my own clothes, and experimented with drawing manga.

At 20, I made a decision: to create music that I genuinely enjoy. Without chasing trends, numbers, or expectations. Even if no one ever listens to it.

In the summer of 2025, I created a track I was truly proud of — ELECTRONICA. That was the first time I felt like I had found my direction.

In February 2026, I came up with the idea for a project inspired by Lapfox Trax (HALLEY LABS) — to create my own internet label where music exists without limitations. That’s how A-CORE PROJECT was born.

This is not just music. This is my own fictional world — with my own rules and my own vision of music.

Here, I separate styles through different aliases and mix everything I love: electronic music, rap, anime, games, and digital aesthetics.

I love music and can’t imagine my life without it. I hope that over time, there will be listeners who understand what I’m trying to express through my music in this trend-driven / type-beat era.
